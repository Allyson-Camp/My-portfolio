About Me

Alexa, play “Beauty School Dropout”. In all seriousness, after spending over 10 years in the beauty industry I knew I wanted to start on a journey to find something I am just as passionate about but would provide a more stable work-life balance. Enter software development! My background as a hair color specialist and makeup artist has taught me how to solve problems using creative solutions. I love to learn the "rules" then come up with my own ways of implementing them while keeping my work clean and concise - just as you need to do as a successful stylist. Having done an apprenticeship for hair and working so closely with a master stylist has also prepared me for working in a team as a developer, which has been paramount to my success up to this point. My time behind the chair also taught me the importance of continuing education throughout my career and always keeping a thirst for new knowledge. After the unfortunate closure of my first coding bootcamp in early 2023 I am determined to keep on my path to becoming an employed developer and am now continuing my Journey at Epicodus in Portland, Oregon.
